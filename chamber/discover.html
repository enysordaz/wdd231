Popular Activities

The Heber Valley is well known for their beautiful surrounding mountains, which give oportunity to multiple activities and places to discover. Some popular recreation and outdoor entertainment is Skiing in the winter season, hiking any time of the year, specially in summer and fall, Kayaking in the beautiful lake, mountain biking, Hot air balloon rides and scenic views. All of this give the opportunity for everyone to enjoy the breathtaking views of Nature.
