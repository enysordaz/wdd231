This Page title represents my in-law family business located in the town I live in with my Husband.

Overview

Purpose

This webpage will have information about their services and options for glasses or products for the customer's aesthetics, such as styled glasses and different scans to manage eye diseases. It will also have the option to schedule an appointment or request more information.

Audience

The main audience is family members. However it is also the people that are looking toward better eye health, etc.

Scenarios

When do I need eye exams/check ups?

What are the best sunglasses for mountain activities?

What are the tools to identify diseases?
